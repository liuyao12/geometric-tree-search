Geometrically Constrained Tree Search

What GCTS Studies

GCTS sits near constraint programming, exact cover, tiling search, and geometric deep learning, but emphasizes the growing search tree itself: frontier choices, symmetry classes of moves, reusable patches, and checkable local certificates.
