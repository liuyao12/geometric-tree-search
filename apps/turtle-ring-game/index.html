Turtle Ring Game

A one-player tiling toy: build a marked turtle corona around a central Trefoil tile, then click a turtle touching it to flip the local pair.

Ready.

Tile name

I suggest Trefoil: the seed has three repeated angle/stripe lobes, and the name distinguishes it from the Smith turtle while hinting at its threefold role.

Game rule prototype

Click a highlighted turtle in corona 1. The app caches the move result by relative Trefoil+turtle position, so repeated positions do not need a fresh brute-force search; if exactly one legal half-turn or reflection exists, only those two tiles move, with a short rotate/flip animation.
