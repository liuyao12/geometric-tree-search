Turtle Ring Game

A one-player tiling toy: build a marked turtle corona around a central Trefoil tile, then click a turtle touching it to flip the local pair.

Ready.

Tile name

I suggest Trefoil: the seed has three repeated angle/stripe lobes, and the name distinguishes it from the Smith turtle while hinting at its threefold role.

Game rule prototype

Click a turtle in corona 1. The app caches the Trefoil+turtle adjacencies that admit a local move, highlights the three currently playable neighbors, and only permits a click when the transformed pair still validates against the surrounding turtle tiling. The Trefoil uses the same blue/orange orientation colors as the turtles.
