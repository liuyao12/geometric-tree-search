Turtle Ring Game

A one-player tiling toy: build a marked turtle corona around a central Trefoil tile, then click a turtle touching it to flip the local pair.

Ready.

Tile name

I suggest Trefoil: the seed has three repeated angle/stripe lobes, and the name distinguishes it from the Smith turtle while hinting at its threefold role.

Game rule prototype

Click a turtle in corona 1. The app tests the actual two-tile union shape by elementary A₂ triangle cells, caches whether that Trefoil+turtle adjacency has a rotational or reflection symmetry, and only permits the move when that symmetry maps the union back onto the same hole without violating the surrounding turtle tiling. Valid neighboring turtles get a thicker border on hover.
